Our Story
WHO WE ARE
🍔

Born in January 2025, Munchies After Dark (MAD) started with one clear mission — to bring premium quality fast food to your door without the premium price tag. We believe everyone deserves a proper, delicious meal no matter the hour.

From our kitchen to your table, every item on our menu is crafted with top notch standards — fresh ingredients, bold flavours, and 100% Halal certified. We don't cut corners. Whether it's our signature MAD Burger or a late-night pasta fix, you're getting the best version of it, every time.
