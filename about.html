How It Started
OUR STORY

Munchies After Dark was born in January 2025 with a simple but powerful mission — to bring premium quality fast food to your door without the premium price tag that usually comes with it.

We noticed a gap in the market: food that either tasted great but cost too much, or was affordable but lacked quality. MAD was built to bridge that gap. We believe everyone deserves a proper, delicious, freshly made meal — no matter the hour, no matter the budget.

As a cloud kitchen, we focus entirely on what matters most — the food and the experience. No distractions, no gimmicks. Just great ingredients, bold flavours and a team that genuinely cares about every single order that leaves our kitchen.

From our signature MAD Burger to late-night pasta fixes and indulgent desserts, everything on our menu is made to satisfy. And with our doors open from 12PM to 3AM every day, we're there when you need us most.
